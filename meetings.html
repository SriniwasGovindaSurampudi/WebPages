Interpreting π's
π's have almost full rank, i.e. if num_regions = 68, then rank is 67.
There are two views, but none has a strong support from cognition point of view.
  • A column of π can be interpreted as a pattern of co-activation of regions. Every column of π is an independent co-activation pattern. But this meaning as 'co-activations' has to be well understood.
    (One link - modulation)
  • According to Ashish Raj's paper, they call a column as initial configuaraion of regional activations. For jth column only region j is active. Hence, is this meaning is extended, jth column of π is a new representation of region j, but this representation is independent but not orthogonal!
